ABOUT
Abacus is a general purpose discord bot whose goal is to cover as many bases as possible. Whether you want moderation tools, autoresponders, custom embeds, or to have fun with images, Abacus has you covered.

Abacus is constantly being worked on, and suggestions are welcome! Join the support server and let us know what you think!
